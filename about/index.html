About Me

I am an experienced engineer passionate about cutting-edge technology and problem-solving. My journey spans from embedded systems to advanced deep learning research.

Currently, I am a Deep Learning Research Engineer at NeuroPixel.AI. Here, I explore and implement innovative AI solutions, focusing on deep neural networks and computer vision.

I began my career at Analog Devices as an Embedded Systems Engineer, where I honed my skills in hardware-software integration and real-time systems.

My academic and research experience includes an internship at RPAD Labs, Carnegie Mellon University, US. During this time, I worked on Active Perception using Light Curtains for Autonomous Driving, advancing the state-of-the-art in perception systems.

My diverse experience has equipped me with a deep understanding of both theoretical concepts and practical applications, enabling me to bridge the gap between research and real-world solutions.
